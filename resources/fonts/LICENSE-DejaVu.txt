DejaVu Fonts
============

The DejaVu fonts are a font family based on the Bitstream Vera Fonts.
Their goal is to provide a wider range of characters while maintaining
the original look and feel through the process of collaborative
development.

Used in Hovera for offline-renderable TTF text in pre-rendered Open
Graph images (1200x630 PNGs streamed by TransporterOgImageController).
Bundled because servers without `fonts-dejavu` installed would otherwise
fall back to GD's built-in bitmap glyphs, which look amateurish for
social-share thumbnails.

Files:
  - DejaVuSans.ttf       — regular weight, used for tagline + footer
  - DejaVuSans-Bold.ttf  — bold, used for tenant name + wordmark
